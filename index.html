Polymer Colour Co.

Manufacturers of Colour Pigments
Polymer Colour Co. was established in the year 1991. We are exclusively devoted to manufacturing and marketing of Pigment formulations. We are the manufacturers of Colour Pigments for FRP (Fibreglass Reinforced Plastics), and Epoxy colour for all types of Plastic.
We have been manufacturing Pigment Pastes used for Unsaturated Polyester Resin Applications (F.R.P), Epoxy etc. since a very long time. Colour preparation is an important factor in highly competitive market and it makes products more beautiful, fashionable and unique. Our pigments run through a series of performance checks before it is delivered to our end users and we use products of consistent quality. Our aim is to provide the best product to our customers while constantly challenging ourselves to offering new and exciting alternatives.

Backed by rich domain experience, we are engaged in manufacturing and supplying an extensive range of Polyester Pigments Dispersions, FRP Polyester Pigments, PU Pigment Paste, Epoxy Pigments, Acrylic Pigments.
